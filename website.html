I am a final year PhD student under Prof. Philip Torr
generously funded by the competitive EPSRC research scholarship
to pursue my PhD in AI for scene understanding at the University of Oxford.

My research covers the broad themes of computer vision, machine learning and deep learning. More fine-grained topics include applied computer vision (for saliency estimation, object recognition, instance segmentation), attention modelling in deep neural nets, analysis of adversariality in deep neural nets.
